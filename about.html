A Legacy of Restored Movement

Our mission is to translate clinical expertise and luxury hospitality into complete physical rehabilitation. Discover our history and facilities.

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones in Skeletal Medicine

Over two decades of expanding clinical reach, integrating advanced systems, and setting benchmark safety outcomes.

2001

Clinic Foundation

Pallawi Hospital founded in Mancherial, Telangana, under the leadership of Dr. Raju Bollabathini to bring advanced musculoskeletal care and state-of-the-art diagnostics to the region.

2010

Robotic Joint Pioneering

Acquired first-generation computerized navigation surgical arms. Performed the state's first outpatient partial-knee replacement under robotic guidance.

2018

10,000 Patient Milestone

Successfully completed our 10,000th surgical procedure. Expanded facility footprint to include a custom high-field MRI diagnostic center.

2026

Luxury Center Upgrade

Completed renovations of our surgical lobbies with Liquid Glass architectural layouts, integrating concierge care desks and post-op rehabilitation environments.
