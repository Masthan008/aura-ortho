Limb Salvage Breakthrough

15cm Bone Lengthening via Ilizarov Fixation

Chief Surgeon: Dr. Raju Bollabathini • Department: Orthopedic Trauma & Reconstruction

The Medical Challenge

A patient presented with a severe leg injury causing 15cm of critical bone loss. Multiple other clinical teams advised immediate leg amputation, deeming the structural damage impossible to recover.

The Surgical Intervention

Dr. Raju Bollabathini proposed and executed an advanced Ilizarov ring external fixation procedure. Over a progressive distraction schedule, the leg bone was systematically lengthened by 15cm, successfully saving the limb from amputation.

"Thankful to Dr. Raju sir for making my mother walk again from a hopeless situation." - Patient's Son
Microvascular Hand Salvage

100% Finger & Hand Movement Restoration

Chief Surgeon: Dr. Raju Bollabathini • Department: Hand & Microvascular Surgery

The Medical Challenge

A patient suffered severe nerve and tendon lacerations in the hand. Prior consultations elsewhere estimated a 90% likelihood of permanent finger immobility.

The Surgical Intervention

Dr. Raju and the Pallawi clinical team executed precision tendon repair and microvascular nerve anastomosis. Within 45 days post-operation, the patient recovered complete (100%) finger flexion and grip dynamics.

"Most doctors said my finger movements were 90% doubtful. Now I have received 100% fingers and hand movements. Hats off to Dr. Raju sir!" - Grateful Patient
