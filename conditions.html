EDUCATION & HEALTH

Patient Resources & Conditions

Learn about skeletal pathologies, download post-operative recovery timelines, and explore diagnostic guidelines.

Osteoarthritis

Degenerative joint disease resulting in wear of articular cartilage, causing friction and skeletal friction pains.

View Care

ACL Tear

Rupture of the anterior cruciate ligament in the knee, leading to joint giving-way during twist motions.

View Care

Carpal Tunnel

Compression of the median nerve in the wrist compartment, leading to tingling, numbness, and grip stiffness.

View Care

Herniated Disc

Displacement of spinal cushion material that pinches local spinal nerves, triggering leg sciatica and spine pains.

View Care

Rotator Cuff Tear

Fraying or rupture of shoulder muscle tendons, causing severe discomfort during overhead reaching movements.

View Care

Plantar Fasciitis

Inflammation of the fibrous ligament banding along the foot sole, triggering acute heel discomfort upon morning steps.

Patient Administration FAQ

Read about appointment guidelines, cancelations, diagnostic scan orders, and post-surgery logistics.

You can request your MRI or CT scan records on CD or secure digital link by filling out our HIPAA Medical Release Form. Please call the records administrator desk at least 48 hours prior to your external second-opinion appointment.
We require at least 5 business days notice for elective surgical rescheduling or cancelations. This allows our surgical coordinators to coordinate cleanroom suites and adjust medical device technician calendar scheduling slots.
Over 70% of robotic-assisted hip and knee replacement procedures at Pallawi Hospital are performed as outpatient surgeries, allowing clients to return to their luxury hotel or home environment the same evening. Overnight clinical stays are only ordered for patients with compound cardiac or pulmonary risk factors.
